# Contributor Code of Conduct

English · [繁體中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-TW.md)

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that supports the community include:

- showing empathy and kindness toward other people;
- respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences;
- giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully;
- accepting responsibility, apologizing, and learning from mistakes;
- focusing on what is best for the project and community;
- protecting credentials, private network information, and personal data shared during troubleshooting.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention;
- trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks;
- public or private harassment;
- publishing another person's private information without explicit permission;
- knowingly publishing credentials, device identifiers, private LAN data, or exploit details that put users at risk;
- sustained disruption of discussions, issues, reviews, or project infrastructure;
- other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code applies in all project spaces and when an individual officially represents the project in public spaces. Project spaces include repositories, issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior should be reported privately to the project maintainer through a contact method published on the [maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/zx90316). Do not use a public issue for a conduct report containing private information.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated on a best-effort basis. Maintainers are expected to respect the reporter's privacy and may remove content, close or lock discussions, reject contributions, issue warnings, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when appropriate.

## Enforcement guidelines

Maintainers may consider the impact, intent, history, and response to feedback when deciding consequences:

1. **Correction** — private explanation and request to correct inappropriate language or behavior.
2. **Warning** — formal warning with conditions for continued participation.
3. **Temporary restriction** — time-limited restriction from project interaction.
4. **Permanent restriction** — permanent removal from project spaces for sustained or severe violations.
